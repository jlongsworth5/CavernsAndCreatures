Game Description:

In Caverns and Creatures, embark on an adventure under the wing of your Lore-Master or with one of our trained and experienced team members at in person or online live events with Ivy Games. Immerse yourself in a magical world that has been enraptured by an ancient curse.

Caverns and Creatures takes players into the winding depths of the buried old world in search of solutions to a blight on the world of Sandalia. Magic once fueled the blossom of a prosperous golden age for the diverse races of the world, but that magic has begun to tear at the world, creating tears in reality allowing denizens of strange and often hostile realms to warp the world around them. Those with access to protective magic retreated into safe isolation leaving most to fend for themselves without the means to combat the changing world.
Journey deep underground with your intrepid companions to uncover the mysteries of the old world in search of the lost history of the old ones. The mysteries of the corrupted ruins of the golden age may hold the key to restoring the world to peace and prosperity.
