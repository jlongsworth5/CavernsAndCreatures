Humans

Human: humans are a diverse group who occupy all levels of society, possessing significant martial, political, and economic power world-wide and able to use magic using totems imbued with their spirit. Humans have a rich and varied history that spans the globe and influences all things in their world with vast empires that each have unique cultures, militaries, and lives. Able to appeal to many across the world, humans are solid all-rounders who can excel in any role.
